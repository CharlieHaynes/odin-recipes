Odin Recipes - Scrambled Eggs Recipe

The perfect scrambled eggs!

This recipe is for the perfect scrambled eggs, whith a creamy, buttery texture
and seasoned to perfection. There isn't a better way to cook them!

Ingredients

  • 4 Large Eggs
  • A Dash of Milk
  • 1 Tbsp of Oil
  • 1 Tbsp of Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Mix 4 eggs and a dash of milk in a small bowl.
  2. Heat 1 Tbsp of oil in a small-medium frying pan.
  3. Add 1 Tbsp of butter to the pan until melted.
  4. Add your mixed eggs from the small bowl into the frying pan.
  5. Constantly fold your egg mixture into itself until it starts to coagulate.
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  7. When you've reached your desired texture, plate up your eggs and enjoy!
