Chicken Fillets Recipe

The perfect chicken fillets!

This recipe is for the perfect, most succulent and
well seasoned chicken fillets!

Ingredients

  • 4 Medium Chicken Fillets
  • 2 Tbsp Salt
  • 2 Tbsp Pepper

Steps

  1. In a bowl, rub the salt and pepper into the fillets, until covered evenly.
  2. Place on a tray in the oven at 160 degrees Celsius for 20-25 minutes, and enjoy!
